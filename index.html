About Me

I am an Informatics Engineering graduate with over three years of experience as a Manual Quality Assurance Tester in the banking industry. I have expertise in test planning, test case creation, and execution, as well as skills in identifying, nalyzing, and reporting bugs to ensure system quality and reliability. I am familiar with testing methodologies such as System Integration Testing (SIT), Functional Testing, Regression Testing, and User Acceptance Testing (UAT). Additionally, I have a basic understanding of API testing using Postman and test documentation with tools like JIRA.

I am always eager to learn and adapt to new technologies. In my role as a QA Tester, I continuously develop my skills to keep up with technological trends, testing methodologies, and the latest tools that enhance testing efficiency. With a strong passion for continuous learning, I strive to contribute my best efforts in ensuring software quality.

Besides my career in QA, I am also a freelance graphic designer working on various projects such as vector art, vexel, caricatures, banners, and more. My interest in graphic design stems from my educational background in Visual Communication Design at vocational school, which shaped my understanding of aesthetics and creativity in design.
